# Blood C 🩸

**Blood C** is a modern, feature-rich Flutter application designed to bridge the gap between blood donors and those in urgent need. With a focus on speed, reliability, and community, Blood C provides a seamless platform for managing blood requests, finding nearby donation camps, and facilitating real-time communication.

**Developed by: Falguni Timande**

---

## 🌟 Key Features

### 1. Dual-Role Experience
- **Donor Home**: A specialized interface for donors to track their contributions, find nearby requests, and view upcoming donation camps.
- **Receiver Home**: A simplified portal for seekers to post emergency requests and locate blood banks quickly.

### 2. Real-time Emergency Requests
- Post and browse live blood requests with details like blood group, hospital location, and urgency.
- Push notifications for donors when a request matches their blood group in their vicinity.

### 3. Integrated Mapping & Location
- Interactive map view (powered by `flutter_map`) to visualize nearby blood banks and donation centers.
- Location-based filtering to find help within a specific radius.

### 4. Real-time Chat System
- Built-in secure messaging platform allowing donors and receivers to coordinate directly without sharing personal contact details immediately.

### 5. Donation History & Recognition
- Digital logbook of all past donations.
- **Gamification**: Earn badges and ranks (e.g., "Life Saver", "Guardian Angel") based on your contribution to the community.
- Digital donation certificates for successful contributions.

### 6. Multi-language Support
- Fully localized experience with support for multiple languages including **English** and **Hindi**, ensuring accessibility for a wider audience.

### 7. Community & Information
- **Live Stats**: Real-time tracking of total donations and lives saved.
- **News & Highlights**: Stay updated with the latest health news and donation success stories.
- **Upcoming Camps**: View and register for upcoming blood donation drives.

## 🚀 How It Works (Application Steps)

1.  **Onboarding**: Start with a beautiful intro screen that explains the mission of Blood C.
2.  **Authentication**: Secure login and registration using Firebase Authentication.
3.  **Profile Setup**: Set your blood group, location, and other health details.
4.  **Role Selection**: Choose whether you are looking to donate or receive blood.
5.  **Taking Action**:
    - **As a Donor**: Browse the "Emergency Requests" section, click on a request, and use the "Chat" feature to coordinate.
    - **As a Seeker**: Create a "New Request," specify the hospital and blood type, and wait for a hero to respond.
6.  **Coordination**: Use the integrated map to find the hospital or blood bank and chat in real-time to finalize the donation.
7.  **Completion**: Once donated, the request is marked as successful, and the donor receives a digital certificate and rank points.
